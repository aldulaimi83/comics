🪨 TARIQ THE IRONWALL 🪨

"The earth stands firm — and so do I!"

  • 🪨 Earth Control — Moves and shapes rock, stone, sand and metal
  • 🛡 Iron Skin — Copper-bronze armor grows from his body on command
  • 🌍 Tremor Sense — Feels vibrations through the ground for miles around
  • ⛰ Terrain Shaper — Raises walls, bridges and barriers from the earth
  • 💪 Immovable Will — When Tariq plants his feet, nothing on earth moves him

Born in the mountain village of Jabal Al-Hadid — "Iron Mountain" — Tariq grew up hearing the old stories about the earth that listens. At fourteen, when a mining collapse buried his father's crew, Tariq pressed both hands to the ground and held up thirty metres of collapsing rock tunnel for twenty-seven minutes until everyone was out.
